## Sprint Retrospective 2026-04-10
### What went well
The sprint in large went well and all items of the sprint were compleated in time before the sprint ended and we achived the results that we wanted. It was a good way of traking and put bounderies on what part of the project we worked on. Pair programing from XP was a good addition for the workflow during this sprint.

### What did not 
When planing the sprint we put up to few items for the sprint and also overesstimated the time of the sprint items. We figured that it was a little to much to have a sprint meeting every day because not all days had us working on items. 
Our branch structure was not as good as it could have been.

### What to change
Moving forward we are still going to work in sprints, but try not to limit them to much so everyone has enough items to work on. Also moving forward we are going to have sprint meetings twice a week on fridays and wednesdays to not have them to repetitivly and perhaps have them more efficently.
